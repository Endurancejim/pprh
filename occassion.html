WEDDINGS

The ultimate destination
wedding venue

For those seeking the ultimate destination wedding, PPHR offers the perfect balance of desert chic and sophistication. One of the only wedding venues in Benin to offer such a versatile selection of ceremony and reception spaces in an atmosphere that is as unique as it is beautiful.

VENUES

A Corporate retreats,
fundraising dinners,
celebrations and more

Encompassing more than 30 acres of property, PPHR offers beautifully landscaped grounds, courtyards and patios, as well as unique indoor spaces to fit any type of occasion. Whether it’s a destination wedding, corporate retreat or meeting, family reunion, birthday or anniversary party, the hotel has a multitude of perfect spaces to accommodate your event requirements.

SPECIAL EVENTS & CORPORATE RETREATS

A versatile selection of venues
for an intimate dinner party,
a large wedding, or anything
in between

The Ritchey Building is the perfect space for a meeting or fundraising event. We can accommodate up to 125 guests in our historically preserved mercantile building. We provide audio/visual equipment plus catering, bar service, staff and planning required to make your event a success. For corporate retreats, we can plan multiple day excursions in the area and help you plan a retreat that your team won’t stop talking about.
